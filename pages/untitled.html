2025. 11m
Filmed over a three month period on Greenpeace’s Rainbow Warrior, untitled(Pacific Ocean) is a short non-narrative moving image work that explores the peripheries of two ocean activist campaigns. The work follows birds to find people, boats and nets on a surging Pacific Ocean. It attempts to make sense of the complex webs of ocean industries, labour exploitation, threatened species, ecosystems and activism.
