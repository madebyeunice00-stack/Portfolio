2025. 16m45s
Filmed over a three month period on Greenpeace’s Rainbow Warrior lll, Somewhere near Norfolk Island and In the Offing: Horomako and Rēkohu/Wharekauri is a short non-narrative moving image work that explores the peripheries of two ocean activist campaigns. The work follows birds to find people, boats and nets on a surging Pacific Ocean.

* Exhibited at Toi MAHARA as part of the Arotake Toi, Mahara Arts Review. 1 November 2025 — 1 Feburary 2026
* Screened at Film Adjacent. September 2025
